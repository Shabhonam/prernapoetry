My Journey in Assam: Family, Inspiration, and Poetry

Assam holds a special place in my heart, not just for its mesmerizing beauty but also because it is where my children were born. My time with my family in Assam was filled with love, laughter, and countless memorable moments. Every day, something around me inspired me to write. Whether it was the peaceful mornings, the gentle rustle of leaves, or the simple joy of watching my kids grow, Assam became the muse for my poetry.

As I navigated my journey at the Oil and Natural Gas Corporation, balancing work and family, the tranquility of Assam provided a space for reflection. This collection of poems is a testament to those cherished days, a blend of personal growth and spiritual connection. Through Prerna, I hope to share not just words but emotions and experiences that shaped my soul.
